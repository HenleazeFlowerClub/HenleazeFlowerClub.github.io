Sallying Forth Once More

We were very lucky to have Sally Taylor kick off the new year flower demonstration series. Just the right tonic for a miserable cold grey January afternoon colourful flowers, interesting designs and sparkling patter. Sally kept apologising for the lack of choice that the flower market offered her but from where I sat it was a cornucopia of colour and variety.

As a seasoned demonstrator, judge and judge trainer Sally generously shared hints and tips, gave valuable insights into how she developed a design and regaled us with anecdotes. Something for everyone judging from the laughter and smiles all round , it certainly lifted everyone’s spirits. With Sally’s permission here are some photos from the demonstration.
